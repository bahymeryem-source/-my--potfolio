About me

Curious mind, entrepreneurial heart

Meryem Bahy is a first-year Computer Science student at the Faculty of Sciences and Technology of Guéliz (Marrakech), expected to graduate in 2027. She is passionate about entrepreneurship, leadership and social innovation.

She founded Bahy Store — an online jewelry brand — and Your Mind Matters, a platform that has already supported more than 20 students with their mental health. She is currently pursuing Harvard's CS50 online to strengthen her computer science foundations.

Career objective: Combine technology, marketing and human-centered design to launch products that create positive impact in Morocco and across Africa.
